How to Make Resume for Freshers

Creating a resume as a fresher can be challenging, but with the right approach, you can build a strong resume.

1. Start with Basic Information

Add your name, contact details, and email.

2. Add Skills

Mention your technical and soft skills clearly.

3. Include Projects

Projects are important for freshers to show experience.

4. Keep it Simple

Use clean formatting and avoid unnecessary design.
